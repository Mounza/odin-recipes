German Meatballs

ingredients for 4 Portions

Ingredients

  • 1kg minced meat (mixed)
  • 2 onions
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bread rolls/buns
  • 1 bunch of parsley (optional)
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • some vegetable fat or oil
  • salt
  • peppe

Directions

  1. soak the bread rolls in lukewarm water. Then squeeze and put it in a bowl. Add the eggs, diced onions, mustard, salt, pepper, and small chopped parsley. Mix it with a whisk.
  2. Now add the minced meat. Knead everything properly. Then form round flat balls (check video for advice).
  3. let the plant fat or oil get hot in a pan. put the meatballs in there and let them fry brown on both sides.
  4. potatoes, mashed potatoes, rice, pasta with a nice sauce all fit as a side dish. good appetite
